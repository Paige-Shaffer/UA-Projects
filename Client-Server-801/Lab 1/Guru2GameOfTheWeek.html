Jeff's Game of the Week

Starfield

Starfield is Bethesda's RPG offering wonderful visuals and a rich universe to explore. With customizable ships, well-engaged combat, and a strong narrative it invites players to chart their own cosmic journey. Despite some minor bugs, it's a must-play for space exploration fans!
